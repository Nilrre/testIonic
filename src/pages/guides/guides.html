The Complete Beginner's Survival Guide

A Proper Backpack

Learn what you'll need to set up camp, how to distribute your backpack's weight, and how well-planned dry food allows you to dine in style while camping.

Finding Your Way

Learn how to utilize everything from shadows to seashells and stars to forge natural navigation techniques in all kinds of terrain.

Making Camp

Learn to tie essential knots, construct a camp kitchen, build shelters to fit your conditions and protect your bed from danger.

Finding Water

Learn how to utilize plants, dig wells, create filtration systems, and even track animals to find and prepare the water you need.

Nature's Cuisine

Learn the ins and outs of catching fish, cooking meat, preserving food, and foraging for plants in ecery environment.

Around the Campfire

Learn how to build a fire pit, craft homespun candles, use the sun as a fire-starter, and recycle campfire ash as a cleaner.

Camp Safety & First Aid

Learn the local landscape, pack smartly, and get to know nature's healing plants to be prepared for any ailment.
